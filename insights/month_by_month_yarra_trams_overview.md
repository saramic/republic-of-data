## Month by month overview of tram performance
Document includes monthly 
* insights of performance
* Weather affects
* New stops and works completed
* Major events affecting traffic
* Outages/ accidents causing interruptions

Source: Railpage - 2014 record performance for trams
http://www.railpage.com.au/news/s/record-tram-performance-in-2014

__May 2018__
- delivered 98.7 per cent of services
- 81.7 per cent of services arriving on time
- new platform stop on Carlisle Street in Balaclava
- improved safety and accessibility on Route 3/3a and 16
- new Master Timetable on May 20

__April 2018__
- delivered 99.1 per cent of services
- 83.9 per cent of services arriving on time
- Best reliability since 2013
- St Kilda Rd Works as part of Metro Tunnel Project
- Hurstbridge Line metro closure as part of Level Crossing Removal Project
- Hoddle Street reduced capacity as part of Streamlining Hoddle Street Project
- successfully delivered 400 additional services on ANZAC Day

__March 2018__
- delivered 98.8 per cent of services
-  78.7 per cent of services arriving on time
- Sydney Rd and Glenferrie Rd Festivals, 
- most challenging week of the year: 
    - Melbourne International Flower & Garden Show,
    - AFL season opening game with the reigning Champions kicking it off at MCG
    - Transporting 230,000 Formula One fans to and from the Grand Prix

__February 2018__
- delivered 98.7 per cent of services
- 81.7 per cent of services arriving on time
- two new platform stops in Park Street, South Melbourne
- Extra services for White Night and St Kilda Festival

__January 2018__
- delivered 98.6 per cent of services
- 87.8 per cent of services arriving on time
- completed important tram track renewal works along Lygon Street, between Weston Street and Elgin Street
- Extra services were provided for Australian Open, during which over 488,000 passenger trips were taken on trams

__December 2017__
- delivered 97.9 per cent of services
- 80.7 per cent of services arriving on time
- extra services for the Boxing Day Test at the MCG as well as on New Year's Eve

__November 2017__
- delivered 97.9 per cent of services
- 79.3 per cent of services arriving on time
- new temperature records including 12 days above 30 degrees, nine consecutive days over 28 degrees and new average temperatures recorded in many places
- Five separate rallies held during the month affected eight tram routes for a total of nearly 22 hours.

__October 2017__
- delivered 98.47 per cent of services
- 82.50 per cent of services arriving on time
- successfully completed tram improvement works on Nicholson Street and Moreland Road in Coburg and Brunswick, and at Royal Park
- additional services to help get our passengers to events across Melbourne including soccer and rugby league matches at Etihad Stadium and AAMI Park, as well as university exams at the Showgrounds and the Cox Plate at Moonee Valley Racecourse


__September 2017__
- delivered 98.55 per cent of services
- 84.48 per cent of services arriving on time

__August 2017__
- delivered 98.48 per cent of services
- 82.89 per cent of services arriving on time
- Additional services were operated in August for a number of special events including 11 AFL matches at the MCG and Etihad Stadium, various expos and concerts, large open days at ACU, RMIT, Monash, University of Melbourne and Rugby League & Union matches at AAMI Park
- successfully delivered works on Lygon Street, as part of our Power Supply Upgrade Project
- delivered improvement works in the Southbank Depot area, including the redesign of accessible Stop 125A on Route 109, in the Fisherman's Bend area

__July 2017__
- delivered 98.69 per cent of services
- 83.88 per cent of services arriving on time
- Additional services were operated in July for a number of special events including 20 AFL matches at the MCG and Etihad Stadium, Disney on Ice at Hisense Arena, 3 concerts at Margaret Court Arena and rugby league and rugby union matches at AAMI Park
- new tram infrastructure on Toorak Road West and St Kilda Road to prepare for Metro Tunnel allowing Route 58 to travel further down St Kilda Rd

__June 2017__
- delivered 98.65 per cent of services
- 83.94 per cent of services arriving on time
- Additional services were operated in June for a number of special events including concerts and AFL, hockey, rugby union, rugby league and soccer matches. Students from RMIT and Victoria University travelling to the Showgrounds for exams also had additional services provided

__May 2017__
- delivered 98.34 per cent of services
- 79.74 per cent of services arriving on time
- Tram operations in May were affected by a number of external incidents. There were four incidents involving trucks in one week - three collisions with trams and one collision with overhead wires. - These included a truck to tram collision which derailed a tram and affected Route 58 services for 10 hours, and a truck which damaged overhead wires and affected Route 82 services for seven hours.
- Additional services were operated in May for a number of special events including concerts and AFL, netball and rugby union matches.

__April 2017__
- delivered 98.30 per cent of services
- 80.90 per cent of services arriving on time
- challenging month for tram operations in part due to unseasonably high rainfall levels. Heavy rain slows down road traffic which affects tram services, as well as causing slippery tracks
- A number of special events also took place during the month which affected services including Run for the Kids and the associated closure of the Domain Tunnel
- Additional services were operated in April for a number of special events, including the ANZAC day Dawn Service, sporting events including the AFL, NRL, A-League and Netball, and concerts at Margaret Court and Rod Laver Arena.
- successfully renewed the intersection at Victoria and Church Streets in Richmond


__March 2017__
- delivered 98.16 per cent of services
- 78.53 per cent of service arriving on time
- challenging month for tram operations due to the number of special events taking place along with some above average temperatures and increased road traffic
- Additional services were operated in March to support the hundreds of thousands of people travelling to special events including the Formula 1 Australian Grand Prix, the start of the AFL season and the Melbourne International Flower and Garden Show

__February 2017__
- delivered 98.56 per cent of services
- 80.32 per cent of services arriving on time
- Additional services were operated in February to support the hundreds of thousands of people travelling to St Kilda Festival and White Night
- Tram improvement works at the Elizabeth Street terminus were successfully completed in February

__January 2017__
- delivered 98.60 per cent of services
- 87.67 per cent of services arriving on time
- Additional services were operated in January for the Australian Open at Melbourne Park to help spectators travel to and from the tennis
- Tram improvement works on Swanston Street near Franklin Street and Swan Street in Burnley were successfully completed

__December 2016__
- delivered 98.75 per cent of services
- 82.00 per cent of services arriving on time
- Tram improvement works on Melville Road, Dawson Street and Lygon Street were successfully completed 

__November 2016__
-  delivered 98.77 per cent of services
- 82.2 per cent of services arriving on time
- High capacity, low-floor E-Class trams are now operating on Line 86, following their introduction on Lines 96 and 11
- Tram track improvement works on Swanston and Collins Streets were also successfully completed

__October 2016__ 
- delivered 98.73 per cent of services
- 83.35 per cent of services arriving on time
- services were affected by rallies in the CBD, third party incidents and traffic from the Tullamarine freeway widening works and Spring Racing Carnival, as well as adverse weather conditions

__September 2016__ 
- delivered 98.94 per cent of services
- 84.5 per cent of services arriving on time
- During September rallies in the CBD, adverse weather, third party incidents and traffic from the Tullamarine freeway widening works and Royal Melbourne Show affected performance outcomes
- additional services for sporting events including for the AFL Finals Series
- Tram improvement works on Line 57 at Errol St were successfully completed

__August 2016__
- delivered 98.82 per cent of services
- 83.32 per cent of services arriving on time
- down on recent performance with major impacts from traffic diverting around the Tullamarine freeway widening works and rolling stock availability pressures due to the retirement of the Z-Class trams
- The typical impact of rallies in the CBD, adverse weather, and third party incidents also contributed to performance outcomes

__July 2016__
- delivered 99.06 per cent of services
- 85.26 per cent of services arriving on time
- rallies in the CBD affected services with trips being terminated short of their destination
- Additional services were provided for a number of sporting events including AFL and the International Champions Cup
- Adverse weather, and third party incidents, also contributed to performance outcomes
- tram improvement works were successfully delivered this month on Flemington Road

__June 2016__
- 98.94 per cent of timetabled services delivered
- 85.41 per cent of services arriving on time
- University exams at the Showgrounds affected the performance of Essendon lines
- freeway accidents and rallies in the CBD affected services from other depots
- Extra services were provided for the university exams as well as for a number of sporting events including AFL and rugby league
- Important tram track renewal works were successfully delivered this month on Whitehorse Road and at Camberwell Junction

__May 2016__
- 98.86 per cent of timetabled services delivered
- 83.68 per cent of services arriving on time
- Incidents during the month that affected services included heavy rain on Thursday 26th during the AM peak
- road traffic in the sporting precinct during events, and road traffic diverting away from freeway upgrade works
- Tram track renewal works on Glenferrie Road at Malvern Depot were completed

__April 2016__
- 98.69 per cent of timetabled services delivered
- 82.6 per cent of services arriving on time
- Flow-on road traffic from freeway widening works continues to affect our services, as did some days of heavy rain and a number of third-party road incidents
- Extra services were provided for a number of sport and concert events throughout the month as well as for the Dawn Service at the Shrine of Remembrance

__March 2016__
- 98.56 per cent of timetabled services delivered
- 83.7 per cent of these services arriving on time
- Tullamarine Freeway Widening has had a notable effect on services since late last year, in particular to Line 59
- extended just before Easter from the Bolte Bridge to Westgate Freeway interchange, there has been significant impacts to Line 59 and 19 which are expected to continue
- Between 12 March to 20 March 2016 Yarra Trams delivered services for a wide range of events  as part of a massive special events season.  On Sunday 20 March tram services were successfully delivered to a number of large concurrent events including Grand Prix, Melbourne International Flower and Garden Show (MIFGS), Run for the Kids, as well as a planned Palm Sunday rally
- The St Kilda Road and Commercial Road renewal occupation were also delivered

__February 2016__
- 98.45 per cent of timetabled services delivered
- 81.91 per cent of these services arriving on time
- third-party road incidents impacted services this month, including a truck rollover at the intersection of Flinders and Spencer Streets, which affected a number of lines on the network
- Major events including White Night and St Kilda Festival were successfully delivered
- Automated announcements and visual displays are now installed on all B-Class trams

__January 2016__
- 98.66 per cent of timetabled services delivered
- 87.51 per cent of these services arriving on time
- improved performance was achieved at a time when two major network upgrades were completed, at Clarendon Street Junction, Southbank and Moonee Ponds Junction
- This year's Australia Open had record attendance, and more high-capacity trams were used to transport passengers to Melbourne Park, with Line 70 seeing an improvement in punctuality
- Night Network was successfully implemented on six lines this month, running all night on Friday and Saturday.

__December 2015__ 98.52 per cent of timetabled services delivered
- 81.65 per cent of these services arriving on time
- mainly affected by increased patronage in the lead up to Christmas, and third-party works and the widening of the Tullamarine Freeway which created increased traffic along some lines
- Days of extreme heat also affected tram operations across the network
- Final preparations for Night Network were completed for the successful launch on 1 January 2016

__November 2015__
- Flooding on Thursday, 5 November caused slow road traffic and subsequent delays to trams, while a power cable falling on tram wires and debris washing onto tracks also presented challenges
- A suspicious package at Southern Cross Station on Tuesday, 17 November disrupted some of our busiest lines for several hours
- a rally with 50,000 attendees on Friday, 27 November blocked services in the CBD in the early evening


__October 2015__
- 98.97 per cent of timetabled services delivered
- 82.64 per cent of these services arriving on time
- mainly affected by the construction of a new level-access platform stop at Wellington Parade, on-road incidents (including collisions with vehicles), the Spring Racing Carnival and third-party works that impacted punctuality on some lines


__September 2015__
- 98.78 per cent of timetabled services delivered
- 85.57 per cent of these services arriving on time
- results were affected by the Industrial action that occurred in September and third-party works that affected punctuality on some lines.
- The roll out of the next generation E-Class trams on the network continues. There are now seven trams in service on line 11 and a total of 28 across the network.


__August 2015__
- 99.12 per cent of timetabled services delivered
- 85.58 per cent of these services arriving on time
- The new platform stops on St Kilda Road between Domain and Southbank Boulevard are now open
- next generation E-Class trams on the network continues, with six trams now in service on Line 11 and a total of 27 in service across the network.


__July 2015__
- 99.23 per cent of timetabled services delivered
- 84.02 per cent of these services arriving on time
- completion of the Blyth Street terminus affected performance this month
